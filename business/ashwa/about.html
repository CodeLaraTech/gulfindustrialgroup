Our Evolution

Innovative Adhesives & Dispersion Engineering

Ashwa is committed to formulating, manufacturing and delivering innovative, high-performance bonding and surface treatment solutions for a wide spectrum of applications. We partner with our customers from inception to offer expert application assistance.

Our specialists bring in-depth knowledge of adhesives, dispensing equipment, and custom formulations to serve customer needs throughout the region. Ashwa continues to set standards in clean-chemical bonding technology.
