Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common inquiries about our global services and support.

What is the typical response time for support? expand_more
Our technical support teams aim to respond to all inquiries within 12 business hours. Priority support agreements feature a guaranteed 2-hour response time.
How do I apply for a partnership? expand_more
Please use the "Partnership" option in our contact form dropdown. Our business development team will review your proposal and schedule an initial discovery call.
Do you provide international shipping and logistics? expand_more
Yes, Gulf Industrial Group handles end-to-end global supply chain operations and custom clearance across five continents from our major regional hubs in the Middle East, Europe, and Americas.
Can GIG manufacture custom specifications? expand_more
Absolutely. Our precision manufacturing and R&D facilities are fully equipped to prototype and execute bespoke mechanical and composite components based on client CAD designs and compliance certifications.
What industries does Gulf Industrial Group serve? expand_more
We specialize in serving high-performance sectors including Aerospace & Defense, Automotive components, Renewable Energy Infrastructure, Smart Logistics automation, and large-scale Construction.
