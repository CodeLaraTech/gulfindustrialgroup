Our Evolution

From Regional Pioneer to Global Titan

In 1957, Gulf Industrial Group was established with a singular vision. What started as a focused regional operation has grown into a multi-national group spanning diverse business segments with synergies through an expansive customer base.

Our journey is guided by "Our Mission Statement" — "To add value in everything we do everyday" — the enduring compass for generations of GIG's founders, leaders and employees as we balance short-term and long-term strategic planning.
